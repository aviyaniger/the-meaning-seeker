Training Courses in Logotherapy

Registered by the Viktor Frankl Institute of Logotherapy and Existential Analysis of Vienna, the Viktor Frankl Institute of Logotherapy in Israel offers online distance teaching in the theory and philosophy of Logotherapy to equip candidates with the expertise to counsel and present workshops and programs in Logotherapy in their different fields of work or professional settings.

Three levels of certification/training

Introductory Level: Personal enrichment, designed for professionals and non-professionals, focusing on the application of Logotherapy to day-to-day life. Includes readings and questions on the readings, interactive discussion, and a short presentation of the concepts at the end.

Advanced Level: Intensive training in the application of Logotherapy in professional settings. Begins with basic tools and techniques and is designed to enhance counseling skills. Includes readings and questions on the readings, interactive discussion, role play practice and case supervision.

Diplomate Level: ​Independent research and further intensive study designed to deepen skills in one’s particular field and to learn to be a trainer and teacher of Logotherapy. Includes a track for clinicians and for non-clinicians.
