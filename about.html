About

The Viktor Frankl Institute of Logotherapy in Israel is an educational and training institute for personal and professional enrichment through the study of Logotherapy.

  • Head trainer: Batya Yaniger, PsyD
  • Trainers:
    • Avraham Friedman, LCSW
    • Dida Kimor, PhD
    • Tanchum Burton, LCSW

The therapeutic approach of Logotherapy (healing through finding meaning in life) was created by psychiatrist, neurologist philosopher and holocaust survivor Viktor Emile Frankl (1905-1997), world famous author of the best seller Manâ€™s Search for Meaning. At the present time it is one of two Institutes that offers a 100% online training program worldwide. Courses take place in small groups, which affords maximum interactive discussion for absorption of the material as well as maximum individual attention in case supervvision. The courses are accredited by the International Association of Logotherapy and Existential Analysis at the Viktor Frankl Institute of Vienna, Austria. Successful graduates are eligible to be registered as Logotherapists and receive a certificate of Accreditation which may be used on official documents.
